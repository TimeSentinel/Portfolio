What Can I Do?

Like most people, I have to many details to list in a single place. If you have something in mind, please send me a message - I'd be happy to go over your request and give you a fair estimate.

Design & Layout
  • UX Design
  • Webpage Creation
  • Stylesheets

From a blank page and a hand drawn wireframe, I can build the site you want or any part of it you need.

Templates & CMS
  • Custom Templates
  • Wordpress
  • Joomla

I can setup a database and design a website with an admin platform to easily manage and change the content and look of your site.

We can also create or modify a Wordpress, Joomla, or other pre-built content management system.

Database & Files

Marketing
  • Keywords
  • SEO
  • Site Links
