A snippit of Lance

A brief summary of where I came from.
My first experiences on computers involved Apple II computers with 5.25" floppy disks and what was called a timeshare computer. The timeshare had no monitor; all output was printed on a 2 or 3ft wide continuous form printer. Not to date myself too much, but this was in grade school for me.
I've had a lot of careers over the years, but the work I love most is design and photography. Web design is a unique environment to work. I can write programs and designs that can be used on nearly every platform.
I am a natural 'learner'. I thrive on learning new things. In between work and exercise, I spend time studying. I love to study languages, programming skills, photography techniques, and what I call 'adventure' skills.
