Case Study: Personal Photo Gallery
A travel journal focused on photos.
Travel Photo Journel

I do a lot of hiking and exploring. I wanted a simple gallery designed to tell the stories of a trip.

Photos organized by date and trip with full page viewing and download functionality.

Gallery of Galleries

I wanted to make a presentation of the entire 'web-book' to captivate and pull people into the stories.

This was fairly javascript and css intensive. Photos would enlarge and display a title with mouse-hover.

Each 'Trip' has it's own primary photo, which then became the background image until another 'Trip' is selected.


All the photos you see in these screenshots were taken by me.

Layouts

I made three formats that each trip could use.

  • The first was a hero-photo followed by smaller tiles.
  • Second was a photo only feed like this one.
  • The final option was a timeline layout which moved horizontally

Personal projects are always a lot of fun when I find time for them. I found that the simplicity of this site came out beautifully.
